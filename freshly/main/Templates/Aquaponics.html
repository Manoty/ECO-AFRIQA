What is an Aquaponics Garden?

An aquaponics garden is a closed-loop system that combines aquaculture (raising aquatic animals) with hydroponics (growing plants in water). In this system, fish waste provides nutrients for the plants, while the plants filter and purify the water for the fish. It's a highly efficient and sustainable way to grow both fish and vegetables.
