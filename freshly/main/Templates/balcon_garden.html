What are Balcony/Rooftop Gardens?

Balcony and rooftop gardens are innovative solutions for urban dwellers who want to enjoy the benefits of gardening in limited spaces. These gardens utilize containers, vertical structures, and other space-saving techniques to grow a variety of plants, herbs, and vegetables.
