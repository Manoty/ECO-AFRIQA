Our Gardens

Hydroponics

Hydroponic Vertical Garden

Hydroponic systems grow plants in nutrient-rich water solutions without soil, often utilizing vertical towers or racks to maximize space. at Freshly towers are used in vertical hydroponic farms to grow a variety of crops, including lettuce, strawberries, and microgreens.

Aquaponics

Aquaponics Vertical Garden

Aquaponic systems integrate fish farming with plant cultivation, where fish waste provides nutrients for the plants, and the plants help filter and purify the water for the fish. Freshly incorporates aquaponics into its vertical farming system, growing greens, herbs, and fish in a closed-loop ecosystem.

Living Walls

Living Walls Garden

Living walls are vertical structures covered with plants, sometimes incorporating hydroponic or soil-based growing systems for urban agriculture.

Vertical Tower Gardens

Vertical Garden Towers

Vertical towers are freestanding structures with multiple planting pockets or shelves, allowing for efficient use of vertical space in urban environments. Freshly installs vertical garden towers in urban areas, providing opportunities for residents and businesses to grow their own food in limited spaces.

Vertical soil-based

Vertical soil-based Towers

Vertical farms that utilize traditional soil-based growing methods but in a vertical configuration, often with stacked planter systems or tiered beds. Freshly operates rooftop greenhouse farms in urban areas, using soil-based growing methods to produce a variety of leafy greens and herbs in vertically stacked beds.

Other

Modular Vertical Garden

Modular systems allow for flexible configurations of vertical growing spaces, often using modular panels or containers that can be stacked or arranged as needed.Freshly Farms Co. offers customizable modular farming units that can be installed in urban environments, providing vertical growing spaces for a variety of crops.

Chicken Tunnels

Chicken Tunnels

Chicken tunnels, also known as chicken runs or poultry tunnels, are enclosed pathways or structures that provide outdoor access and roaming space for chickens while protecting them from predators and other hazards
